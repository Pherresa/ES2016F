About the game

When you start a new play, you have to take the most important decision, play with the Mordor horde or thr Rohan's army.
Depending on the decision you take, you will have one game or another

This game is a Tower Defense, your objective is that the enemy hordes do not destroy your main tower, to achieve this, you have to learn to play with the terrain and the economy, to be able to put defense towers on the map.

Are you ready?
